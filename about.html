About Me

Alexander Schüßler

I am a PhD candidate and researcher at the Reconfigurable Robotics Lab, EPFL. My research focuses on reconfigurable morphology and intelligence in robots for adaptable human augmentation. My vision is to realize human-centered robots that seamlessly integrate with humans and their environments to improve human capabilities and support human flourishing.
