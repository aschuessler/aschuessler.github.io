Alexander Schüßler is a PhD candidate and researcher at the Reconfigurable Robotics Lab, EPFL. His work intersects soft matter and embedded machine learning to create human-centered, transparent interfaces for physical human-robot interaction. His translational efforts focus on affordable and effective healthcare technologies.
